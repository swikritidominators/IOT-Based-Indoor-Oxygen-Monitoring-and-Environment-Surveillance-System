============================================================
8. AIR-CONDITIONING EFFECT ANALYSIS
============================================================

Statistical Test:

Mann-Whitney U Test


Controlled Occupancy Range:

2 - 4 occupants


AC ON:

Mean O2 Depletion Rate

0.000495% per 5-minute interval


AC OFF:

Mean O2 Depletion Rate

0.004106% per 5-minute interval


Statistical Significance:

p-value = 0.0032

Result:

Statistically significant at p < 0.01.


Interpretation:

Within the physics-informed synthetic dataset, AC-on periods
showed a lower mean oxygen depletion rate than AC-off periods.

This result validates that the statistical testing pipeline can
detect the AC effect encoded in the synthetic data generator.

It should not be interpreted as a general empirical conclusion
about real-world air-conditioning systems.

============================================================
10. LIMITATIONS
============================================================

1. All experiments were conducted using physics-informed
   synthetic data.

2. Real-world sensor noise, sensor drift, cross-sensitivity,
   and environmental confounding variables may affect model
   performance.

3. The dataset represents a single simulated room configuration.

4. Occupancy values used during the experiments were simulated.

5. Model performance must be re-evaluated after sufficient
   real sensor data is collected.
